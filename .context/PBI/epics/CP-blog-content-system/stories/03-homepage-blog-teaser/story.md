# Homepage Blog Teaser

**Epic:** Blog Content System
**Priority:** Medium
**Status:** Backlog

---

## User Story

**As a** homepage visitor
**I want to** see recent blog posts without navigating to /blog
**So that** I discover the blog exists and can jump to content that interests me

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Homepage shows a "Latest Posts" section with 2-3 most recent blog posts
- [ ] Each teaser shows title, date, and a short excerpt
- [ ] Clicking a teaser navigates to `/blog/[slug]`
- [ ] Section is visually consistent with existing homepage layout
- [ ] Automatically stays in sync with available MDX posts

---

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Blog teaser section visible on homepage
- [ ] Click-through works for all featured posts
- [ ] Build passes
